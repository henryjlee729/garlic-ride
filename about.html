Our mission is to revolutionize the ridesharing industry.

GarlidRide was built on the idea that fast, convenient, and cheap ridesharing services should be available to everyone (only for Purdue-affliated people, no IU people allowed!). We aim to help people on the West Lafayette campus and beyond to make ridesharing very convenient.

Our Founding

GarlicRide was a project developed by 4 guys who met up for the 2023 Hello World Hackathon. We are committed to building a decent project for the hackathon.

Growth & Beyond

We do not have any plans to expand this project as it was only a project idea. This is only meant to be a fun project made by 4 guys.
